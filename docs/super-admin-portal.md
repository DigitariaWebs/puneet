# Super Admin Portal - Comprehensive Feature Set

## 1. Dashboard & Overview

### Centralized Dashboard

- **Multi-Facility Overview**: Real-time view of all facilities in the system
- **Key Metrics Display**:
  - Total number of active facilities
  - Total reservations across all facilities
  - Overall system revenue
  - Active users count (facilities, staff, customers)
  - System health status
- **Performance Charts**:
  - Revenue trends over time
  - Facility growth rate
  - Customer acquisition trends
  - Reservation volume trends
- **Quick Actions Panel**:
  - Add new facility
  - View recent activities
  - Access support tickets
  - Send global announcements

### Facility Performance Monitoring

- **Individual Facility Dashboards**:
  - Reservation statistics per facility
  - Revenue generated per facility
  - Customer count and growth rate
  - Staff utilization rates
  - Occupancy rates
- **Comparative Analytics**:
  - Compare performance across facilities
  - Benchmark facilities against each other
  - Identify top and bottom performers
  - Monthly/yearly performance comparisons
- **Visual Data Representation**:
  - Charts showing facility performance
  - Graphs for revenue tracking
  - Customer acquisition rates (monthly, yearly, etc.)
  - Reservation volume visualization

## 12. Customer Relationship Management (CRM)

### Lead Management

#### Lead Capture

- **Lead Entry Form**:
  - Facility/Business name
  - Owner/Contact person name
  - Email
  - Phone number
  - Business address
  - Lead source (website, phone, referral, event, etc.)
  - Current business size (approx. number of pets they handle)
  - Services they offer (boarding, daycare, grooming, training)
  - Notes/Comments

#### Lead Status Pipeline

- **Pipeline Stages**:
  - New Lead
  - Contacted
  - Demo Scheduled
  - Demo Completed
  - Proposal Sent
  - Negotiation
  - Closed Won (converted to customer)
  - Closed Lost
- **Visual Pipeline**:
  - Drag-and-drop board to move leads between stages
  - Shows number of leads in each stage
  - Total potential revenue per stage

### Sales Management

#### Deal Tracking

- **Deal Information**:
  - Expected subscription tier (Beginner/Pro/Enterprise/Custom)
  - Modules interested in
  - Estimated monthly/annual value
  - Expected close date
  - Probability of closing (%)

#### Activities & Follow-ups

- **Activity Log**:
  - Log calls, emails, meetings with timestamp
  - Add notes from conversations
  - Set follow-up reminders
  - Task assignment to sales team members

#### Sales Analytics

- **Basic Metrics**:
  - Total leads in pipeline
  - Conversion rate per stage
  - Average deal size
  - Average time to close
  - Win/loss ratio
  - Revenue forecast

### Contact Management

#### Contact Database

- **Contact Details**:
  - Name and title/role

  - Facility association
  - Decision maker (yes/no)
  - Communication history
  - Tags/categories

#### Facility Profile (Pre-Customer)

- **Business Info**:
  - Facility name
  - Business type
  - Location
  - Number of staff
  - Approximate capacity
  - Current software/system they use
  - Pain points/needs

#### Email Integration

#### Task & Reminder System

- **Conversion Process**:
  - One-click convert from "Closed Won" to active facility
